Our story

Check-Up was created to address the everyday challenges faced by basketball players, trainers, fans, and enthusiasts. By connecting the game we love in real-time, we aim to revolutionize the basketball community under one platform. Inspired by the lessons the game has taught us, we believe Check-Up, with the support of the community, is the future of sports.

Our Mission
To connect and celebrate the basketball community in real-time.

Our Vision
To create a global basketball marketplace that encompasses the entire hoop culture.

Our Plan
To build a thriving social network tailored for the basketball ecosystem.

2017
Started as a college senior project, evolving into our MVP on the App Store
2020
Redesigned our MVP, offering users expanded features including more courts, a newsfeed, Check-Up TV, and check-ins, amassing our initial 5000 users.
2022
Updated the app, introducing ScoreCam, hosting Check-Up events, creating content, and partnering with the basketball community, resulting in a community of over 30,000 members.
2024
Completely recoded the app for both Apple and Android, unveiling brand new tools, enhanced features for the feed, courts, events, and more, with exciting developments on the horizon.
